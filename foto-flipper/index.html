Photo Flipper

A powerful yet simple photo editing app for iOS. Flip, rotate, crop, straighten, and apply filters to your photos with intuitive swipe gestures.

Features

  • Flip photos horizontally or vertically with a natural swipe gesture
  • Rotate images in 90-degree increments
  • Crop to custom dimensions or preset aspect ratios
  • Auto-straighten using Apple's Vision framework
  • Apply a variety of photo filters
  • Import from Camera, Photo Library, or Files
  • Undo and redo support
  • Available in 16 languages
