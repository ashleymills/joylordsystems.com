Privacy Policy

Photo Flipper — Effective date: 1 January 2024

Joylord Systems ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Photo Flipper mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

Photo Flipper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your photos are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the App.

Photos and Camera Access

The App requests access to your device's camera and photo library solely to allow you to edit photos. All image processing is performed locally on your device. We do not access, collect, or store any of your photos.

Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to help us understand how the App is used and to improve it. This data includes:

  • App opens and screen views
  • Feature usage (e.g., which editing tools are used)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Country/region (derived from IP address, which is not stored)

This data is collected in aggregate and cannot be used to identify individual users. For more information, see Google's Firebase Privacy Policy.

Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports. These reports help us identify and fix bugs. Crash reports include:

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Stack trace information from the crash
  • A random installation identifier

Crash reports do not contain any personal data or information about your photos.

Advertising

The free version of Photo Flipper displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain data to serve relevant ads. You can learn more about Google's advertising practices at Google's Advertising Policies.

If you purchase the premium version, all ads are removed.

In-App Purchases

Photo Flipper offers in-app purchases to unlock premium features. All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting
  • Google AdMob — advertising (free version only)

Children's Privacy

Photo Flipper does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The App does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support page.
