Secret Santa

The easiest way to organise your Secret Santa gift exchange. Add participants, set a spending limit, and let the app do the rest.

Features

  • Add and manage participants for your gift exchange
  • Random assignment with exclusion rules
  • Set spending limits to keep things fair
  • Share assignments via text, email, or messaging apps
  • Simple, festive interface
